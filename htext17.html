OUR JOURNEY

17 Years of Milestones

From humble beginnings to international presence, our journey has been defined by vision, perseverance, and community impact.

2008

The Beginning

Gtext Homes is born in a single-room office in Lagos. "If the roof leaks, we fix it—if the dream leaks, we double-seal."

2010

First Estate Launch

First estate launch, Emerald Garden City. Proof of concept → proof of courage.

2013

First Billion

Crossed ₦1 billion in sales. Mom finally stops worrying.

2016

National Expansion

Expansion to Abuja & Port Harcourt. From one city's skyline to three.

2018

Gtext Cares Launch

CSR arm Gtext Cares feeds 100K people. Impact > income.

2020

Digital Transformation

Digital pivot during pandemic; 70% of sales go online. "Zoom calls, zero excuses."

2023

Global Expansion

Dubai & London offices open. Passport stamps ≠ limits—just checkpoints.

2025

17th Anniversary

17 countries served, 50+ estates delivered. The world is now our neighbourhood.
